Our Family Olive Farm

The olive oil used in our soaps comes from our family olive farm in Sparta, Greece. This farm has been passed down through our family for centuries, carrying with it generations of tradition, care, and connection to the land.

Every bar of Lathi Soap is rooted in that heritage and made to bring a piece of Greece into your everyday routine.
