About Lathi Soaps

Our story begins in Greece, where her grandfather hand-pressed olives each harvest. Inspired by those simple, honest methods, Lathi Soaps continues the tradition: slow, cold-processing bars that preserve the natural goodness of olive oil.

Each bar cures for weeks to develop a mild, long-lasting lather. We operate entirely online — no physical storefront — and ship directly to you.
