Our Story

At Lathi Soap, we believe the best ingredients come from family traditions. We are a local, family-owned business creating handmade olive oil soaps with care, quality, and heritage at the heart of every bar.


The olive oil used in our soaps comes from our family olive farm in Sparta, Greece. This farm has been passed down through our family for centuries, carrying generations of tradition and connection to the land.


Every bar of Lathi Soap is inspired by our Greek roots and made to bring a simple, natural piece of Greece into your everyday routine.
